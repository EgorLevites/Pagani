Pagani Zonda

The Pagani Zonda is a mid-engined sports car produced by the Italian manufacturer Pagani. It debuted in 1999, and production ended in 2017 with the Zonda HP Barchetta.

The Zonda's design was inspired by jet fighters and the Sauber-Mercedes Silver Arrow Group C cars. Its distinctive styling and high-performance capabilities have made it a favorite among car enthusiasts worldwide.
