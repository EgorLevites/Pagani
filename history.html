Pagani Cars History

Explore the evolution of Pagani supercars over the years.

Pagani Zonda

The Pagani Zonda is a mid-engined sports car produced by the Italian manufacturer Pagani. It debuted in 1999 and has undergone various iterations and special editions over the years.

Pagani Huayra

The Pagani Huayra is the successor to the Zonda and was introduced in 2012. It features a twin-turbo V12 engine and incorporates advanced aerodynamics and engineering.

Pagani Utopia

The Pagani Utopia is the latest creation from Pagani. Unveiled in 2022, it represents the pinnacle of automotive design and engineering, blending futuristic aesthetics with cutting-edge technology.
